The keyframe will be store the attributes of the objects as such basically

Timestamp, Camera, Light, Rider, Bike

Camera :
1. Type
2. If the type is global :
i. Position, Up, Rotation
Else, garbage values to keep the record length same

Light :
1. Positions of global lights
2. States of all the lights

Rider, Bike :
1. Global translation coordinates of the Rider and Bike
2. Euler Angles of the rotation matrices according to the hierarchy defined earlier
